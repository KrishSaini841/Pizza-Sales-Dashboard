# Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project provides a comprehensive business intelligence report on pizza sales for the year 2015. The objective was to analyze revenue trends, identify top/bottom-performing products, and optimize order processing.

## Tech Stack
* **Analysis & Querying:** SQL (Microsoft SQL Server)
* **Visualization:** Tableau
* **Data Sources:** Pizza Sales Database

## Key Business Insights
* **Revenue Drivers:** Identified high-revenue categories and the specific pizza names driving the most sales.
* **Peak Performance:** Discovered peak ordering hours (12 PM–1 PM and 4 PM–7 PM) and busiest weeks (48th week of the year).
* **Product Optimization:** Recommended menu adjustments based on the "Bottom 5" sales performance data.

## Features
* **KPI Banner:** Tracks Total Revenue, Average Order Value, Total Pizzas Sold, and Total Orders.
* **Trend Analysis:** Hourly and Weekly visualizations to assist with staffing and inventory management.
* **Category/Size Performance:** Pie and bar charts showing the contribution of specific pizza categories and sizes to overall revenue.
